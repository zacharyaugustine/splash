Three must-have fonts

[Tex Gyre Heros](http://www.fontsquirrel.com/fonts/tex-gyre-heros) - recreation of Helvetica [Crimson](https://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Crimson+Text) - Garamond-inspired with all the modern fixings [Open Sans](https://www.google.com/fonts/specimen/Open+Sans) - Myraid-inspired with multi-lingual character sets
